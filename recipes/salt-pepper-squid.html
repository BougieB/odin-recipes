Salt and Pepper Squid

Description

Caught some fresh squid? Here's the best way to prepare it!

Ingredients

  • Fresh squid tubes and tenticles
  • 500ml neutral oil
  • 2 tsp five spice
  • 1/2 cup of whole milk
  • 1 cup of plain flour
  • 2 eggs
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a deep pan to 180*c
  2. Mix 5 spice, salt and pepper into the flour and put into a low walled tray
  3. Mix the eggs and milk until well combined
  4. Dry the squid completely, then dredge in the flour, followed by egg mix, followed by flour again
  5. Gently put dredged squid into the oil and cook until golden brown
  6. Dry on paper towel and immediately season with salt
  7. Serve immediately
